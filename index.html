So how to download a specific commit from gitHub ?

I saw lots of people asking how they can download a specific commit of a project from github. lets browse to commit history or commit list page of your project

Then click to any commit you want to download

so here you can see the "sha" blue highlighted, just copy that

so your download link should be in this syntax- https://github.com/{username}/{projectname}/archive/{sha}.zip
